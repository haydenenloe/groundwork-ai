## Logo

### Icon Mark
Three stacked horizontal bars, left-aligned, decreasing in width from bottom to top.
Metaphor: geological strata / foundation layers / building up from the ground.

- Bottom bar: full width, 100% opacity
- Middle bar: ~70% width, 72% opacity
- Top bar: ~40% width, 44% opacity

### Wordmark
"Groundwork" in Inter Bold (700), body text color  
" AI" in Inter Bold (700), primary blue (#4B7FFF)

### Spacing
Icon mark to wordmark: 12–14px gap

### Don't
- Don't separate the icon from the wordmark in small contexts — use icon only below ~120px wide
- Don't change the bar proportions
- Don't use on non-dark backgrounds (no light-mode version yet)

## Typography

**Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- Display/Headlines: 700–900 weight, tight tracking (`tracking-tight`)
- Body: 400 weight, relaxed leading
- Labels/Badges: 600 weight, wide tracking (`tracking-widest`), uppercase, small size
- Code/stack labels: Monospace, small, on dark background

---

## Voice & Tone

**Direct.** No filler. "We build AI systems" not "We help organizations leverage AI to achieve their goals."

**Builder, not consultant.** We talk like operators, not strategists. Output, not deliverables.

**Honest about scope.** We say who this is NOT for. That builds trust.

**Specific.** Pricing on the page. Timelines on the page. No "contact us for pricing."

### Copy Rules
- Never: "leverage," "synergy," "unlock potential," "transform your business"
- Always: concrete outcomes, specific timeframes, named tools
- Use "we build" not "we provide"
- Use "production system" not "solution"
- Use "your team" not "your organization"

---

## Visual Style

- Dark mode only (for now)
- Subtle dot/grid background on hero section
- Blue radial glow in hero and CTA sections
- Card borders that glow blue on hover
- Clean white space — don't crowd
- SVG icons, no emoji in interface elements (emoji OK in callout blocks)
- No gradients except the text gradient on the hero headline and the blue/blue-light button hover
